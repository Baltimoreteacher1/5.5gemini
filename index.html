Akela's Shipping Box

Akela has a box with a volume of 830 ⅞ in³.

Width = 8 ½ in, Height = 8 ½ in.

Shipping rules: The box must be no longer than 12 inches.

Step 1: Calculate Base Area

"First, multiply Width × Height to find the Base Area (B)."

Step 2: Solve for Length

V = B × L  →  L = V ÷ B

Step 3: Check Constraint

Is the length 12 inches or less?

"Akela can use the box because 11 ½ is less than 12."
